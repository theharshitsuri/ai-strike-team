# REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL (RFP)
# Project ID: 2025-BRIDGE-04
# Title: River Elm Bridge Structural Rehabilitation

## 1. Overview
The Department of Transportation (DOT) is seeking proposals from qualified engineering firms to perform a comprehensive structural rehabilitation of the River Elm Bridge (ID: EB-104). The bridge is a critical 240-foot four-lane crossing that has shown significant masonry and joint degradation.

## 2. Scope of Work
The selected contractor will be responsible for:
- Full structural assessment and load testing.
- Replacement of all expansion joints (8 total).
- Concrete resurfacing and waterproofing of the main deck.
- Installation of new high-visibility LED safety lighting.
- Scour protection for bridge piers 3 and 4.

## 3. Submission Timeline
- RFP Issued: September 1, 2025
- Mandatory Pre-bid Meeting: September 15, 2025
- **Submission Deadline: November 15, 2025 at 5:00 PM EST**
- Anticipated Award: December 20, 2025
- Construction Start: March 15, 2026

## 4. Financial Terms
- **Estimated Project Budget: $4,200,000**
- Bid Bond: A 5% bid security is required for all proposals.
- Payment: Monthly milestone payments based on certified progress.

## 5. Technical Requirements & Qualifications
- Firms must hold a valid State Grade A Engineering License.
- Lead project manager must have 15+ years of experience in bridge rehabilitation.
- All materials must meet DOT Standard Specifications Section 4.2.1.
- Compliance with the EPA Stormwater Permit for construction activities (NPDES) is mandatory.

## 6. Insurance & Compliance
- General Liability: $5,000,000 per occurrence.
- Workers Compensation: Statutory limits.
- Compliance with FAR 52.204-7 (System for Award Management).
